How it works

Four laps, one pace.

Every job search has the same stages. RolePace runs them as a loop, not a one-time push — so your pipeline stays full without you living in job boards.

Lap 1

Match

We search live openings and score fit against your approved skill inventory — not keyword guesswork.

Lap 2

Tailor

Your resume is tailored per role, grounded strictly in what's on your approved profile. Nothing gets invented or rounded up.

Lap 3

Apply

Applications go out through direct-employer platforms like Greenhouse, Workday, and Lever — the ones that don't put your other accounts at risk.

Lap 4

Track

Every application, reply, and interview lands in one dashboard, with reminders so a good lead never quietly goes cold.

Why trust it

No fabrication. Ever.

Automated applying only works if what goes out the door is true. So we built the gate before we built the automation.

Your skills, reviewed before they count

Every skill that can appear in a resume or affect a job match first passes a human-reviewed approval step.

Grounded tailoring only

Resume tailoring pulls exclusively from your approved skill inventory. If it's not approved, it doesn't get written.

No title inflation

We don't stretch delivery or IC experience into executive titles to chase a match. Your history stays your history.

You can see every send

Every automated application, message, and edit is logged and visible to you — nothing happens in the dark.

Where we apply

Direct to the employer, not through a third party.

We automate on the ATS platforms companies actually hire through, and deliberately stay off networks where automated activity can put your account standing at risk.
